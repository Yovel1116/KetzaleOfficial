Ketzale is an Israeli duo creating and playing techno and afro-tech music.
At the beginning of their career, the two brothers were part of an alternative rock band and performed in a number of performances abroad.

At present, Ketzale are mainly focused on creating techno and afro-tech music and have recently collaborated with producer Leo Guardo and South African singer Nomvula to release a single called Heart Healers on Connected.

Ketzale goal is to create fine music and bring cool vibes among the underground community with their music.
